About Me

Hi, I’m Matt Tedesco, a passionate developer with a lifelong knack for problem-solving. My journey with technology began early—at just 4 years old, I built my first computer. This experience ignited my curiosity and taught me the value of tackling challenges head-on, a mindset I carry into every project today.

With hands-on expertise across macOS, Windows, and Linux, I bring a versatile approach to development. Whether it’s designing intuitive front-end experiences or crafting robust back-end systems, I thrive on finding creative solutions to complex problems.

Currently, I’m sharpening my Python and Java skills through Codecademy’s interview prep courses, adding to my foundation in full-stack development. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me experimenting with home server setups or exploring ways to simplify everyday tasks through technology.

Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
