About Me

Hi, I’m Matt Tedesco, a passionate developer with a lifelong knack for problem-solving. My journey with technology began early—at just 4 years old, I built my first computer. This experience ignited my curiosity and taught me the value of tackling challenges head-on, a mindset I carry into every project today.
